About The Band



Current Biography

Under The Den formed in the autumn of 2014 as a collaborative effort by four people trying to make good music.
Madison Lewis, the group's lead vocalist, hails from Orange County, CA. A voice all her own,
she pours her soul into live performances with an audience large or small.
Benjamin "Benzo" Zoleski plays guitars. A music major from Berklee, Ben brings a great range to
the guitar and vocals along with an amazing positive energy and stage presence. The rhythm section is comprised of identical twin
brothers John Nowak (on bass) and drummer Jason "J NO" Nowak. Both have a long musical history
in the 518 and abroad, with bands on indie and major labels. Playing originals alongside a host of select cover tunes, this group hopes to
capture the positive essence and momentum of the progress which has become symbolic of the Upstate NY area.
Our Mission: To entertain, enrapture and enlighten audiences who enjoy dancing to great music. The band actively supports many causes of good nature including:

  • Military Causes:
    • Veterans Groups
    • Active Military Charities
    • Rock Therapies
  • Child Life Enrichment
    • Boys and Girls Clubs
    • STEM programs
    • Music Schools and Education
  • and Progressive Businesses like:
    • Transfinder
    • Deathwish Coffee
    • The Low Beat
    • Lark St. Business Community

Madison Lewis

Madison Lewis keeps busy inside the music industry and out.
She is constantly volunteering time to help in local charities, support local business,
and check out others in the great upstate music scene. You can see her at Albany's
"The Barn" artist lofts, judging musical competitions locally, or recording with other
artists and friends. She enjoys a great brunch and spending time with family, and is a little
too nerdy about Star Wars. Her major musical influences include Prince, Shania Twain, Brand New, and Guns N Roses.

Benzo

Ben is the youngest in the band, but he has a rich background
playing many shows with many different types of bands inlcuding The Refrigerators
and C.K Flach. He enjoys teaching music when not rocking or creating, which is why he graduated from Berklee School of Music with a degree in Music Instruction.
Ben was recently picked up as a Musical Director and player for the touring production
of Andrew Lloyd Webber's "School of Rock", and will be joining the band again in 2019.
Ben plays exclusively Moniker guitars, Marshall Amps, and D'Addario strings. His major
musical influences range from B.B King, to Micheal Jackson, to Hawthorne Heights. He also loves spending time with his cat and a good New York A.P.A.

John Nowak

John Nowak has been playing bass for a number of years.
Writing much of the material, and driving the pace of the music, his
sense of the dancefloor from being a Drum n Bass DJ ("SOLO") shines in
the thumping "4 on the floor" grooves in tracks like "All That Talk" or
"The Spark". His history of rocking decks as well as the bass allows
for more musicality in the band. His influences range from Coheed and Cambria
to outlaw Country like Willie, Waylon, and David Allen Coe, to Teenage Bottle Rocket,
David Bowie, John Digweed, and much more. John is also heavily involved with
Malcolm's Restaurant in Schenectady.

Jason "JNO" Nowak

Jason Nowak has been playing drums around the capital region and abroad for many years. He started in the hardcore/punk scene of Upstate Ny in the late 90's playing for most notably Endicott (Equal Vision Records). Since then he has gone on to play for many other projects of various genres. Jason also spends time coding and has recently returned to college to pursue a BS in Computer Programming. Jason plays Truth Drums and loves his Paiste cymbals. His musical influences would be Candiria, the Police, Daft Punk, and many more.
